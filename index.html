Excel And Inventory Management

"In a world full of data and calculation, Excel is an easy solution." -Sumaiya Akter Annie

What is inventory management? Inventory management is the management of inventory and stock. As an element of supply chain management, inventory management includes aspects such as controlling and overseeing ordering inventory, storage of inventory, and controlling the amount of product for sale.

Excel Benefits: Excel is an application which is very helpful in organizing and storing huge amount of data records in a very short amount of time. By using this application, we can sort out the inventory list according to date, name etc. We can also build a lookup function to search for any inventory in a very huge list and find its stock, delivery quantity etc. We can build a pivot table for easier lookup process and sorting. Graphs, eg. Pie charts, histogram or line graphs can be used to represent the statistical conditions, and provide a better visual of the whole data.

In conclusion, the process helped us to: 1. Calculate the right amount of inventory to stock. 2. Know the reorder point for new inventory. 3. Figure out the right place to sell and distribute my inventory. 4. Understand the income and expense from sale and manufacture of an inventory.

Excel is an easy store and calculation of large amount of data with just a few formulas and functions. Plus they are organized. Tired of all the works? Sit back. Keep calm and excel!
